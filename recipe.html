Ingredients

1 chicken breast (cut into small chunks)

1/4 normal sized store bought broccoli (finely chopped)

1/4 white onion

2heaped tablespoons of basil pesto (below instructions on how to make home-made)

spaghetti pasta

5rosa tomatoes

Method

1Heat some olive oil in a frying pan.

2Fry your onion and chicken, seasoning with some salt and pepper.

3Once browned, add your finely chopped broccoli.

4Whilst frying, season with chilli flakes/paprika.

5Add your basil pesto.

6Fry for about another 5-10 minutes until the whole mixture looks slightly brownish and crispy.

7Serve above mixture on a bed of spaghetti pasta, accompanied by some fresh rosa tomatoes cut in halves. Delish!
