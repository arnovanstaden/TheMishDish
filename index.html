About Mish

My name is Mish. I am not a professional chef and I have no education or background in either cooking or nutrition. I am merely your every day gal with a passion for cooking and creating dishes and ‘recipes' which are: affordable, easy to make, my personal opinion of healthy, made from your every day fridge ingredients and full of flavor. I often find that recipes by well known chefs, despite being delicious, were either too difficult to follow, required specific kitchen tools, or required ingredients which you had to go out and source, buy at a high price and then never use again. That is why I created ‘The Mish Dish’ - a personal catalogue of some of my favorite, personally created, go-to dishes. Enjoy
